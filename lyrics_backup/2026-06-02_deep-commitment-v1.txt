[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire burning when our shadows start to blend
A quiet kind of magic that I never want to end
I trace the little constellations hiding in your eyes
We lose ourselves tonight beneath the midnight skies

[Verse 1]
The world outside our window is a thousand miles away
We leave behind the heavy rush and let the moments sway
I pull you close and feel the steady rhythm of your chest
In this soft and tender hour, I have found my perfect rest

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is pulling us a little closer now
There is nothing left to hide, we do not need a vow
The silence is a melody that only we can hear
I just want to hold you in this atmosphere

[Chorus]
This is a deep commitment written in the dark
A quiet kind of gravity that lights a sudden spark
I am giving every piece of me, nothing to deny
You are the only reason I am reaching for the sky

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Nothing to deny)
I am yours until the end of time

[Verse 2]

We let the embers burn in a captivating glow

[Bridge]
The shadows fade but this remains, a promise softly spoken
A sacred kind of silence that will never once be broken
